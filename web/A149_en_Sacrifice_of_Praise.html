Sacrifice of Praise (A149)
Marcus Meier
1. Let  me not waste a moment of this life.
On things that are  passing. On things that are fading away.
Help me not let a minute pass me by.
Without  thinking of    You. Without falling deeper in love.

Pre-Chorus: You make my soul   sing
You make my heart come alive   
You are my portion in this life    

I'll bring a sacrifice of praise
Into the house of Your sweet name
I'll bring an offering that's pleasing to Your heart
I give You praise that's rightly due
Laying down all to follow You
I'll bring an offering that's pleasing to Your heart   

2. How can I trade the winnings of this life
For the excellence of knowledge, the wonder that I   may gain Christ.  
Help me to run the race to win the prize
Of that   which You've purchased
Of that which You've laid hold of me   

I'm wanting all of You. I'm wanting all of You, Oh Lord.
