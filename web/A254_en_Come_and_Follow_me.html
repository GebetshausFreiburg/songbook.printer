Come and Follow me (A254)

1. We are marching to the beat  of different drum
To the beat of a perfect heart, here today
We are marching on a road  to a distant land
and I can see the Saviors hand reaching to me

2. We are sailing on a ship  on a crystal sea
And I can see our destination in the air
It is rising like a castle in the sky
And its filled with heavens light everywhere

And I can hear Him say,  'Come and follow me'
'I will take you there,  come and follow me'
'Leave it all behind,  come and follow me'   
   I can hear Him calling out my nameme'

3. I can hear it like the sound of a mighty rain
Like the sound of a diesel train headed for me
I can feel it like the trembling of the ground
And I know that I am bound for eternity

4. He is coming like the flash of a neon light
He will tear apart the sky when He appears
When we see Him we will finally be like Him
We will finally see Him clearly just as He is
