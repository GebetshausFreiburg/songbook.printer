Love is (A194)
Kevin Prosch
1.What if I spokewith the tongues of men and angels, but had no love:
I would becomea     noisy gong or a clanging cymbal
If I could prophesyand know all mysteries   and all knowledge
If I had faith, the  kind of faith to move mountains
But if  I don't have the kind   of love, the love it takes       -   I am nothing
And if  I give all my     possessions to    the poor,       it  profits me nothing

But love is patient, love is kind;    your love does    it every time    
Now abides   faith, hope and love;    but the greatest of these is love    


2.Love suffers long and it is kind, it is not jealous,
It thinks no evil, it     does not rejoice in iniquity 
Love never fails,nor  seeks its own  but always is giving
I need grace to    walk in this love, Lord
'Cause it bears all things, believes all things
And hopes all things,      endures all things
And what I need is a whole lot more of this pure love       to show the world

That love is patient, love is kind;    your love does    it every time    
When my heart    begins to cry,      your love does    itevery, every   time
