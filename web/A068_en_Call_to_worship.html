Call to worship (A068)
Matt Redman
1. We come to your mountain,
The hill of the Lord; we would ascend,
And journey into   Your holy   place.     
2. To   feast in Your presence, and bring our devotions to       You, God;
We come as a kingdom of       Your priests.

We're climbing up the mountain of the Lord,
Towards Your holy place, And every step is praise.
Encountering the glory of Your name,
Your throne of holiness, The wonders of Your grace.


3. We enter Your sanctuary,
To   minister at   Your holy   throne,
Where thousands of angels joyfully sing.


Bridge:
Oh come, come let us worship,
Come let us recognize what a sacred thing we do. 
Come, let us bow down, God;
As we bring    our hearts, let them please the heart of You.
