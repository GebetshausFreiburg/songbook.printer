We Welcome You (S167)
Danny Daniels
1. We  are here together 
To call upon Your name. 
There is nothing we like better   
Than to sing and give You praise.

(Lord) ('Cuz) we welcome You, we welcome You,
we welcome You, Come fill  this place.       

2. Bring healing and salvation,
Let Your kingdom come. 
Right here just like in heaven
May Your will be done.

Bridge  
Father come fill  this place,
We welcome You.
Jesus we seek  Your face,
'Cause all we want to do
Is give our love to You.

Ending  Come fill this place.
