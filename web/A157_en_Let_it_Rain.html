Let it Rain (A157)
Johannes Eiwen
Let it rain!   Let it rain   on me!
Let it rain,   o Lord!

1. O Lord, hear my cry, see the dry    land of my life
O Lord, fill me up with your water of life!     

2. O Lord, hear my cry, let the river  flow in me 
O Lord, quench my thirst with your water of life!     
Ending
Let it rain, oh Lord.
