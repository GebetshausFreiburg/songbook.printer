Light of your face (A033)
Misty Edwards
Oh Lord bless me and keep me, Cause your face to shine on me. Lord be gracious, lift the light of your countenance. Give me peace.
For I       live only to see your face, so shine on me. (2x)
Let the light of your face shine down on my heart and let          me   feel       it.
